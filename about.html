About Us

We are the team that brings that de-javu effect!

Why and how we started

We lovve writing, we love our planet, we love you that sums it all up.

We started out as a team of enthusiastic nerds behind our keyboards writing our interests and passions.

What we solve and we solve it.

Ignorance is a dangerous situation, so we strive to keep you out of it. We write to equip you with information

When you are equipped with information and come to know it personally, it becomes power you wield.

Our promise

To deliver accurate, timely, ancient and futuristic information that can become your knowledge

To make this world a better place through our articles not spreading hate and fake news.
